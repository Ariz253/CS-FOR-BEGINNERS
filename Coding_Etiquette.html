Coding Etiquette

Few tips to follow for effective coding.

Learn How to Learn

  • Develop effective learning strategies, such as focused and diffuse thinking, to enhance your coding skills. Consider taking courses like "Learning How to Learn" on platforms like Coursera.

Start Small

  • Begin with basic syntax and gradually tackle more complex challenges. Engage in coding exercises that build your confidence and understanding before moving on to larger projects.

Understand Programming Fundamentals

  • Familiarize yourself with key concepts such as algorithms, data structures, loops, and functions. A solid grasp of these fundamentals will make advanced topics easier to understand.

Choose the Right Language

  • Select a programming language that aligns with your goals. You can follow the sources that we have provided to learn your first programming language.

Engage with Communities

  • Join online forums or communities such as Stack Overflow. These platforms provide support, resources, and opportunities to ask questions when you're stuck.

Document Your Learning

  • Keep a coding journal where you document challenges, solutions, and insights. This practice not only reinforces learning but also serves as a valuable reference in the future.

Seek Mentorship

  • If possible, find a mentor who can provide guidance and feedback on your work. Having someone experienced to turn to can significantly accelerate your learning process

Read Documentation

  • Try to make a habit of reading documentation on various programming languages along with video lectures. Many professionals agree that documentations are also one of the best sources to learn a programming language.
