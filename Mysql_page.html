  • Codecademy offers a comprehensive interactive course on SQL that covers fundamental concepts and more advanced topics. Khan Academy

  • Khan Academy features an engaging course titled "Intro to SQL: Querying and Managing Databases." This course combines video tutorials with interactive coding challenges, making it accessible for learners at all levels. The platforms structured approach allows learners to progress at their own pace while receiving instant feedback on their exercises.

SQLBolt

  • SQLBolt is an interactive online platform that teaches SQL through a series of lessons and exercises designed for quick learning.
